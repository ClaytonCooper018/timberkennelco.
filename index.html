About Timber Kennel Co.

Welcome to Timber Kennel Co., a family-run basset hound breeding program nestled in the heart of Texas. Our kennel is home to Timber, our loyal and loving sire, and our two sweet girls, Lucy and Ellie. Together, they make up the heart of our operation—and our home.

As a big family with lots of little hands and big hearts, our puppies are raised in a warm, kid-friendly environment, making them incredibly well-socialized and ready to become part of your family. We’re passionate about breeding healthy, happy bassets with the long ears, soulful eyes, and easygoing charm the breed is known for.

Join the Timber Kennel Co. legacy—where quality meets compassion, and every basset hound is raised to thrive in your home and your heart. When you choose a Timber Kennel Co. pup, you’re not just getting a dog—you’re gaining a loyal companion, raised with intention, integrity, and the warmth of a true Texas family.
